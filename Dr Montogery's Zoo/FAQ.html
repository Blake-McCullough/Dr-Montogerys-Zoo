  • Basics

  • Where abouts are you?

    We are located at 59 Irvine St, Sandstone WA 6639

  • How much does it cost?

    The cost is $20 for adults, $10 for children and concession card users.

  • Can I pay by card?

    We support card and cash, we do not support cheques

  • Do you have a gift store?

    We do have a gift store at the exit.

  • Animals

  • Are the animals real?

    The animals are 100% real.

  • How did you create the animals?

    We cannot tell you that as then we would be sharing out secret!

  • How many animals have you got?

    We have over 100 animals!

  • Events & Passes

  • Do you have discounts for schools?

    We have a discount of up to 25% of the cost when you have 25 or more student come.

  • Do you do parties?

    Yes we do! We have 2 party rooms and have lots of activites for the kids to do!

  • Do you close on public holidays?

    We do not! We only restrict our hours to 9am to 5pm

  • Do you have season passes?

    Yes we do have season passes, They are $75 and offer full access up to 50 times!

  • Other

  • How do I get a job here?

    We are not currently hiring but feel free to drop in your resume and cover letter to the front counter.

  • Is it safe?

    All animals are kept behind double layer thick fences and glass for your protection.

  • How do I get their with public transport?

    You can have a look on the TransPerth website and on google maps to find out how.
